General Questions

What services does Routivate offer?

We offer comprehensive IT services including website design & development, cloud infrastructure, cybersecurity, digital marketing, e-commerce solutions, and IT consulting. Our team specializes in creating tailored solutions for businesses of all sizes.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, e-commerce platforms 4-8 weeks, and enterprise solutions 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups and have extensive experience helping new businesses launch their digital presence. We offer flexible engagement models and can help with MVP development, investor presentations, and scalable architecture planning.

Technical Questions

What technologies do you use?

We use modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Angular, Node.js, Python, Java, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and various databases. We choose the best stack for each project based on requirements, scalability needs, and long-term maintenance.

Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive hosting solutions with 99.9% uptime guarantee and ongoing maintenance packages. Our maintenance includes security updates, performance optimization, backups, and 24/7 monitoring.

How do you ensure website security?

We implement multiple security layers including SSL certificates, regular security audits, vulnerability scanning, DDoS protection, and compliance with industry standards (HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS). All code undergoes security review before deployment.

Pricing & Process

What's included in your pricing?

Our pricing includes design, development, testing, deployment, and initial training. Maintenance and hosting are available as separate packages. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden costs and detailed breakdowns of all services.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we offer various support packages from basic maintenance to comprehensive 24/7 support. Our support includes bug fixes, performance optimization, security updates, and feature enhancements based on your evolving needs.

What's your development process?

We follow an agile methodology with regular updates and iterations. Our process includes discovery, planning, design, development, testing, deployment, and ongoing support. We maintain transparent communication throughout the project.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We integrate seamlessly with existing teams and can work as an extension of your in-house developers. We adapt to your preferred communication tools, workflows, and development practices.

48-Hour MVP Questions

How is 48-hour delivery possible?

Our 48-hour MVP service leverages pre-built components, streamlined workflows, and our experienced team working in shifts across time zones. We focus on core functionality first, ensuring your MVP is market-ready quickly.

What's included in a 48-hour MVP?

A complete, responsive website with core functionality, contact forms, basic SEO, mobile optimization, and deployment. Additional features like e-commerce, user authentication, or complex integrations may extend the timeline.

Is the quality compromised for speed?

Not at all! We maintain the same quality standards by using proven methodologies, experienced developers, and thorough testing. Our 48-hour delivery focuses on efficiency, not cutting corners.

Getting Started

How do I start a project?

Contact us through our website or email. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your needs, provide recommendations, and prepare a detailed proposal. No obligation until you decide to move forward.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we respect your intellectual property and are happy to sign NDAs before discussing project details. Your ideas and business information are completely confidential.

What if I need changes after project completion?

We understand that businesses evolve. We offer flexible retainer agreements and hourly rates for post-launch changes, enhancements, and new features. Many clients choose our ongoing maintenance packages for continuous improvement.
